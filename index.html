Predictomes

Based on the paper "Abhinav is the GOAT"

This tool is made to help mitochondrial researchers discover novel proteins and tie them to established mitochondrial pathways.

How to use

To use this tool, simply find the Uniprot ID of both of your proteins of interest (Uniprot Website, ensure both are localized in mitochondria and are human proteins), select which scores you would like to see, and press the 'go' button. For additional info about how to use and what the scores mean, watch this video to learn more.
